## What is Wizado?

Wizado is an interactive learning platform designed to make education fun and engaging for children. Big brains, little legends - through interactive activities, games, and challenges, kids can explore multiple subjects at their own pace while earning rewards and tracking their progress.

---

## Our Purpose

We believe learning should be **engaging, fun, and accessible**. Wizado transforms traditional education into an interactive experience where children:

- 🎮 Learn through interactive games and activities
- 🎯 Build confidence through immediate feedback
- 📈 Track their progress and achievements
- 🏆 Earn streaks and rewards for consistency

## Learning Subjects

### 🔢 Mathematics

- **Identify Numbers** - Recognize and speak numbers aloud
- **Counting Basics** - Master counting from 1 to 20
- **Multiplication Tables** - Learn and practice multiplication facts
  - _Learn Mode_ - Study tables step-by-step
  - _Practice Mode_ - Test your knowledge with interactive quizzes

### 🇮🇳 Hindi

- **हिंदी की गिनती (Hindi Counting)** - Learn to count in Hindi
- **हिंदी संख्याएँ (Hindi Numbers)** - Learn written number forms
- Audio pronunciation available for all content

### 🧠 General Knowledge

- **Reading Clocks** - Learn to tell time on analog clocks
- **Letter Recognition** - Identify and learn letters

### 🔬 STEM

- **Units of Measurement** - Understand different measurement units (length, weight, time, etc.)

### 🌍 World Geography

- **Flags of Countries** - Identify flags from around the world
- **Capitals of Countries** - Learn capitals of countries worldwide
- **Capitals of India** - Explore Indian state capitals
- **Flag Challenge** - Test your knowledge with a timed challenge

## How to Use LearnQuest

### Getting Started

1. Visit [wiz.poovi.in](https://wiz.poovi.in/)
2. Select a subject from the main screen
3. Choose a course or activity
4. Start learning!

**No registration, downloads, or complicated setup needed!**

### Learning Modes

**📚 Learn Mode**

- Step-by-step guidance through the material
- Perfect for first-time learners
- Move at your own pace

**🎯 Practice Mode**

- Test your knowledge with interactive quizzes
- Multiple choice questions
- Immediate feedback on your answers

**⚡ Challenge Mode**

- Timed challenges for fun competition
- See how many questions you can answer correctly
- Perfect for building speed and confidence

### Features

✨ **Immediate Feedback** - Know right away if your answer is correct

🎊 **Celebration Effects** - Confetti, streak badges, and rewards for correct answers

🎤 **Audio Support** - Hear proper pronunciation of words and numbers

📱 **Play Anywhere** - Use on phones, tablets, or computers

⏱️ **Your Recent Activity** - Quick access to courses you've been working on

🕐 **Learn at Your Pace** - No time limits, learn whenever you want
